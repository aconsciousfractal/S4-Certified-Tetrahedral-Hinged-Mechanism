in `docs/PAPER_TO_ENGINE_TRACEABILITY.md`, `docs/SOURCE_LOCK.md`, and
`docs/EXPERIMENT_LEDGER.md`.

- `certified/a7c_selected_hinge_contact_side_certificate_manifest.json`: selected-hinge signed-orientation records.
- `certified/b04_a7c_contact_side_bridge_manifest.json`: six-row B04/A7c bridge.
