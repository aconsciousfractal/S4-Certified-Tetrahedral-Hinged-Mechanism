# Theta-star external red-team scope

This extension asks for mathematical review of the finite-atlas proof chain,
not review of fabrication, CAD, STL, or physical prototype behavior.

A reviewer should check:

1. whether the 108-tree finite atlas and 18-representative reduction are stated
   with the correct scope;
2. whether the class predicate separates full-open, positive-jam, and
   instant-jam cases without semantic overclaim;
3. whether exact theta-star values and first-contact witnesses are supported by
   imported artifacts;
4. whether transport from representatives to all 108 trees is explicitly
   justified or clearly marked as an obligation;
5. whether no physical, positive-thickness, three-parameter, or non-equal
   theorem is being claimed.
