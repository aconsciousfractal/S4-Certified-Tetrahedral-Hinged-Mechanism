
# Normalization lock

All claim classes in this package use the claim-level convention:

- CL5: theorem candidate or proof-level mathematical claim.
- CL3: certified finite/digital artifact claim.
- CL1: computational evidence or process evidence.
- Metadata/process: reproducibility, manifest, and packaging claims.

RW12 artifacts are normalized as digital fabrication-review files only.  They must not be normalized upward into physical evidence without new measurement records.
